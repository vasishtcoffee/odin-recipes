Description

Pav Bhaji is one of Mumbai's most iconic street foods, loved for its spicy, buttery, and flavorful taste. It's a delicious blend of mashed vegetables cooked with a special mix of spices, topped with a dollop of butter, and served hot with soft pav (bread rolls) toasted in butter.

Ingredients

FOR BHAJI

  • 2 tbsp butter (plus more for serving)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped or pureed
  • 1 capsicum (green bell pepper), finely chopped
  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled & mashed
  • 1 cup cauliflower, boiled & mashed
  • 1 cup green peas, boiled
  • 1 carrot, boiled & mashed (optional)
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped (optional for spice)
